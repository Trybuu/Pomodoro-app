What is Pomodoro?

Pomodoro tehnicue

It,s a method of time menaging, developed by Francesco Cirillo in 80s. It help's to be more productive. Name Pomodoro comes from kitchen watch wich looks like tomato. Base of this tehnicue is to work/learn in parts. Each part is 25 minutes long, after it there's a 5 minutes break. Cirillo proved that this it helps to stay focused and keep mind fresh.

How to use this app?

Firstly decide what you want to focus on. Add your tasks into the tasks list by typing it into task input and click plus icon. If you got more than one task choose which one you want to focus on first. Then click the start icon under the timer clock and work! If you make it in first 25 minutes click on the icon on the right side from your task to set it as a finished task, if not make a 5 minutes break and continue for next 25 minutes until you make this. Good luck!

Tips to make pomodoro more effective

  1. Inform your roomates/familly that you are going to study.
  2. Turn off your phone.
  3. If there's something that helps you to focus make it! For example listen to music.
